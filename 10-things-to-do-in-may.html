Best Things in Laughlin, NV in May 2025

Here's Why May is a Great Time to Visit Laughlin, NV

Warming Weather:May in Laughlin brings warmer but still enjoyable temperatures—average highs around 91°F (33°C) and lows near 65°F (18°C). It’s the sweet spot before the triple-digit summer heat, making mornings and evenings ideal for outdoor fun along the Colorado River.

River Experiences: With water temperatures rising, May is the perfect month to cool off with:
Laughlin River Tours’ Sunset Dinner Cruise: Enjoy a three-course meal aboard the Grand Celebration boat.
Jet Boat Tours: Experience a thrilling ride to Lake Havasu, passing through the Havasu National Wildlife Refuge.

Outdoor Activities: The favorable April weather is perfect for:
Grapevine Canyon Petroglyphs: Hike among ancient Native American rock art just a short drive from town.
Big Bend of the Colorado State Recreation Area: Offers opportunities for picnicking, fishing, and wildlife observation.
Laughlin Labyrinths: Explore stone mazes that provide scenic views of the river and surrounding landscape.

Live Entertainment: May 2025: The concert scene heats up this month with big names:
The Marshall Tucker Band: Performing at The Edge Pavilion on May 10th, 2025.
Big & Rich featuring Gretchen Wilson: May 24 at Laughlin Event Center.
Ongoing Live Bands Aquarius Resort’s Splash Lounge and Tropicana’s Tango’s Lounge.

Gaming and Nightlife: Try your luck or enjoy cocktails riverside. Most Laughlin casinos offer: Slot machines, table games, and poker rooms, late-night dining and live music, and rooftop bars and drink specials!

Scenic Golf Courses: May is perfect for early-morning tee times: Laughlin Ranch Golf Club offers stunning desert-mountain views and a spa for post-round relaxation, or El Rio Golf Club, which is in nearby Mohave Valley, is also a local favorite with a challenging layout.
