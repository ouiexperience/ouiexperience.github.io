Best Things in Laughlin, NV in March 2025

Here's Why April is a Great Time to Visit Laughlin, NV

Pleasant Weather: April in Laughlin offers pleasant temperatures, with average highs around 82°F (28°C) and lows near 55°F (13°C). This makes it a comfortable time for outdoor activities before the intense summer heat arrives.

Water-Based Experiences: Take advantage of the serene Colorado River with:
Laughlin River Tours’ Sunset Dinner Cruise: Enjoy a three-course meal aboard the Grand Celebration boat.
Jet Boat Tours: Experience a thrilling ride to Lake Havasu, passing through the Havasu National Wildlife Refuge.

Outdoor Activities: The favorable April weather is perfect for:
Colorado River Heritage Greenway Park & Trails: Ideal for walking, biking, and birdwatching.
Big Bend of the Colorado State Recreation Area: Offers opportunities for picnicking, fishing, and wildlife observation.
Laughlin Labyrinths: Explore stone mazes that provide scenic views of the river and surrounding landscape.

Live Entertainment: April 2025 boasts a variety of entertainment options:
Grand Funk Railroad: Performing at The Edge Pavilion on April 5, 2025.
Village People: Live at The Edge Pavilion on April 19, 2025.
Scotty McCreery: Headlining at The Edge Pavilion on April 26, 2025.

Gaming and Nightlife: Laughlin's array of casinos and entertainment venues provide vibrant nightlife options, including live music, comedy shows, and gaming experiences.

Scenic Golf Courses: Golf enthusiasts can enjoy several courses with picturesque views, suitable for all skill levels.
