Best Things in Laughlin, NV in November 2025

Here's Why November is a Great Time to Visit Laughlin, NV

Milder Fall Weather: In November, Laughlin’s desert heat has mostly cooled. Average highs reach the upper 60s °F (around 20 °C), and lows dip into the 40s °F (around 5–10 °C) by month’s end — perfect for exploring without the intense summer heat.

Outdoor & River Fun: The Colorado River is still beautiful this time of year, offering relaxed river walks, mild paddleboarding, and scenic water-taxi rides. Cooler temperatures make it ideal for sightseeing or enjoying a riverside lunch.

Scenic Hikes & Nature Spots: Take advantage of the cooler weather for outdoor adventures:
• Grapevine Canyon Petroglyphs: Hike among ancient Native American rock art just a short drive from town.
• Big Bend of the Colorado State Recreation Area: Enjoy hiking trails, fishing, and wildlife viewing.
• Heritage Greenway Trail: A paved riverside path that connects Laughlin’s casinos to nearby viewpoints.

Golfing & Relaxation: Early mornings are perfect for tee times at Laughlin Ranch Golf Club — known for its desert-mountain views and on-site spa. El Rio Golf Club in nearby Mohave Valley is another local favorite for golfers seeking a challenge.

Live Entertainment: November 2025 brings exciting performances to Laughlin’s top venues:

  • Jason Aldean — Laughlin Event Center, November 8, 2025.
  • Cher Live! The Tribute — Don Laughlin’s Riverside Resort, November 12–16, 2025.
  • Live and Let Die: The Music of Paul McCartney — Riverside Resort, November 19–23, 2025.
  • Local Favorites: Nightly live bands and lounge acts continue at Aquarius Casino’s Splash Lounge and Tropicana’s Tango’s Lounge.

Gaming & Nightlife: Try your luck riverside — Laughlin’s casinos stay lively all month with slots, table games, poker rooms, and late-night dining. Enjoy drink specials, rooftop bars, and music while overlooking the river.
