Best Things in Laughlin, NV in July 2025

Here's Why July is a Great Time to Visit Laughlin, NV

Mid-Summer Heat:July in Laughlin turns up the heat with average highs around 108°F (42°C) and lows near 82°F (28°C). The intense midday sun makes early mornings and late evenings the best time for outdoor adventures. Hydration and shade breaks are key!

River Experiences: : With the Colorado River offering a refreshing escape from the heat, July remains ideal for water-based fun:
Laughlin River Tours’ Sunset Dinner Cruise: Enjoy a three-course meal aboard the Grand Celebration boat.
Jet Boat Tours: Experience a thrilling ride to Lake Havasu, passing through the Havasu National Wildlife Refuge.

Outdoor Activities: The extended daylight hours are great for:
Grapevine Canyon Petroglyphs: Hike among ancient Native American rock art just a short drive from town.
Big Bend of the Colorado State Recreation Area: Offers opportunities for picnicking, fishing, and wildlife observation.
Jet Ski Rentals & Paddleboarding: Cool off on the water with high-speed jet skis or take a more relaxed pace with paddleboarding near the Harrah’s or Riverside launch points.

Live Entertainment: July 2025 brings even more energy to the Laughlin stages:
ZZ Top: Rocking the stage at The Edge Pavilion on July 13, 2025.
Dierks Bentley: Performing under the stars at Laughlin Event Center on July 27.
Local Favorites: Nightly performances continue at Aquarius’s Splash Lounge and Tropicana’s Tango’s Lounge all month long.

Gaming and Nightlife: Try your luck or enjoy cocktails riverside. Most Laughlin casinos offer: Slot machines, table games, and poker rooms, late-night dining and live music, and rooftop bars and drink specials!

Scenic Golf Courses:Early mornings are ideal for tee times: Laughlin Ranch Golf Club offers stunning desert-mountain views and a spa for post-round relaxation, or El Rio Golf Club, which is in nearby Mohave Valley, is also a local favorite with a challenging layout.
