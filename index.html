FAQ

Why should you go off-roading with a guide?

Going off-roading with a guide ensures safety, access to local expertise, and knowledge of the best trails, wildlife, and terrain. Guides can also handle unexpected situations, making the experience more enjoyable and stress-free.

What is the minimum age required to drive a UTV with OUI Experience?

21 with a valid drivers license.

Can the driver and passenger share drivetime?

Absolutely! As long as everyone is 21 or older!

What should I bring or wear while preparing for my off-road trip?

You dont need to bring anything, but you should dress accordingly with the weather. Bring swimsuits during summer and early fall. You will get DIRTY! Don't wear nice clothes! We recommend closed toed shoes and pants. We provide water, but you are allowed to bring non-alcoholic beverages and food!

Why is a private UTV experience the ultimate experience?

Flexible & Customizable: Set your own pace, choose your favorite stops, and explore the terrain your way—no rigid itineraries here.
Exclusive Experience: Avoid crowds and distractions. Enjoy the serenity of nature and breathtaking views with just your group.
Unforgettable Memories: We capture your adventure with free photos, so you’ll have lasting memories of your thrilling day.
No Interruptions: Unlike group tours, you won’t risk having your trip cut short due to others' behavior—it’s your private adventure, on your terms.
