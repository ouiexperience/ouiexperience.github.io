Best Things in Laughlin, NV in December 2025

Here’s Why December is a Great Time to Visit Laughlin, NV

Milder Winter Weather: In December, Laughlin’s desert climate delivers comfortable daytime highs around the low-60s °F (about 17 °C) and nighttime lows in the mid-40s °F (about 7-8 °C). The chill is gentle, making it a great time for exploring without extreme heat or deep cold.

Outdoor & Riverside Relaxation: With cooler, pleasant temperatures, the riverside atmosphere along the Colorado River is perfect for leisurely paddleboarding, scenic water-taxi rides, or just relaxing at a riverside resort lounge. Even if water activities are less intense than peak summer, the relaxed pace and scenery make it worthwhile.

Nature, Hikes & Fresh Air: Take advantage of milder weather for outdoor adventures:
• Grapevine Canyon Petroglyphs: A moderate hike among ancient rock art near town.
• Big Bend of the Colorado State Recreation Area: Offers gentle trails, wildlife viewing, and a scenic river-backdrop.
• Heritage Greenway Trail: A paved riverside walking/biking path ideal for cooler mornings or afternoons.

Festive Live Entertainment: December brings seasonal and major shows in Laughlin:
• The Wilbury Super Group: A Tribute to their Greatest Hits — Dec 3, 2025 at Don Laughlin’s Riverside Resort.
• Righteous Brothers — Dec 6, 2025 at The Edge Pavilion.
• Cowboy Christmas – Tribute to Brooks & Dunn and Reba — Dec 17 & 18, 2025 at Don Laughlin’s Riverside Resort.
• LeAnn Rimes — Dec 20, 2025 at The Edge Pavilion.
• Local nightly acts: Riversiders can still enjoy live music lounges, gaming, and riverside nightlife at venues like the casinos and lounges along the Colorado River.

Gaming & Holiday Nightlife: The casino scene remains vibrant: slot machines, table games, poker rooms, late-night dining, and festive specials and live music create an inviting evening atmosphere in the cooler season.
